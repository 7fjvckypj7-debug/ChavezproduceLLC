About Chavez Produce LLC

A Family Legacy, Grown for Global Scale

Chavez Produce LLC is a family-run avocado grower and exporter based in Jalisco, Mexico — operating with the standards, transparency, and reliability of an enterprise-grade export partner.

Our Commitment

Quality and Partnership at Every Stage

For generations, our family has cultivated Hass avocados using sustainable practices and modern packing standards. We work closely with buyers to ensure every shipment meets the expectations of U.S. importers, distributors, wholesalers, retailers, and foodservice suppliers.

Our Story

Three Generations of Growth

First Generation

Planting the First Orchards

Our family began cultivating avocados on a small plot in Jalisco, focused on quality fruit for local markets.

Second Generation

Expanding the Operation

Acreage expanded and modern agronomy practices were introduced, improving yield consistency and fruit quality.

Third Generation

Building for Export

Chavez Produce LLC was formed to formalize export operations, partnering with approved packing facilities and logistics providers to serve U.S. buyers.

Today

A Trusted Export Partner

We continue to grow our network of U.S. importers, distributors, wholesalers, retailers, and foodservice partners — built on consistency and trust.

Our Mission

Premium Fruit. Lasting Partnerships.

To provide premium Hass avocados to U.S. buyers while building long-term partnerships based on trust, transparency, and operational reliability — at a scale that serious produce businesses can count on.
